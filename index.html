Mikka Azores About

Mikka Azores

Mikka is a 22-year old Multimedia Designer who moved all the way from sunny Philippines to Canada eight months ago in order to further her skills and knowledge in design.

Back in the Philippines, she studied Multimedia Arts for four years and worked with Maybelline as a Multimedia Design consultant. She got to create both online and offline advertisements and collaterals for them, with an animated billboard being her biggest project. Amidst everything, she also does freelance web design and video.

In between the moments she spends on her laptop, you can find her reading a book, getting mad at the Philippines' drug war, crying about missing her dogs, eating ice cream, spending time with friends, and documenting her life through writing, photos, and videos. Videos most of all, which she loves compiling into one movie.

Kateh Mayman About

Kateh May

Kateh Mayman is a 27-year-old graphic designer and digital illustrator who relishes opportunities to bring art and textiles into her design work. Kateh graduated from Fanshawe's Graphic Design program and then settled into the flexibility of freelance life. Though she was born in London, ON, she has also claimed residence in Brussels, Belgium and Vancouver.

Kateh has worked with clients such as Big Brothers Big Sisters, London Health Sciences Centre, Sony, Armor Games, Curiobot, and Clearbags Canada. Chasing after the video game industry in order to pursue her passions, Kateh has an appetite for interactive design and art that video game design has to offer.

You can frequently find her glued to a video game or napping. She is very fond of the stories the world has to offer: devouring books, news articles/feeds, podcasts, audio books, and, of course, video games. She also very much likes cats.
